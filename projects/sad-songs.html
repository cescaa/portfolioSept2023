Sad Songs for Summer


(HTML / CSS)

Scroll on image to see full page.

Using HTML and CSS, I created a webpage dedicated to my favourite song of 2022 (and my sad summer). Through the illustrations I curated and modified and the overall design, I wanted users to connect with my work without needing to read it. My goal was to convey the juxtaposition between the vibrancy and lightness of the summer season and the sadness and pain I experienced during it.

My Tools and Languages
  • HTML/CSS
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Photoshop
Design Process

If design and development have one thing in common, it's the iterative process. Creating beautiful and functional work requires time and involves trial and error. To arrive at my final design, I had to experiment with and explore various aesthetics and layouts.

In this iteration, I experimented with portraying angry, "ugly" emotions.
