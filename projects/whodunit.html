Whodunit Wordplay


(HTML / CSS / JS)

The main page of Whodunit Wordplay.

Inspired by my love for cozy mysteries like "Only Murders in the Building" and "Knives Out," I created an enhanced version of the classic game Hangman. In this new iteration called "Whodunit Wordplay," players are tasked with deciphering a secret keyword by guessing words or individual letters, but what sets my version apart is that players have a time constraint of just three minutes to crack the code. Additionally, for every incorrect guess, 10 seconds are deducted from the remaining time.

Functionality

  • A random keyword is selected among an array of cozy mystery-themed words for every new game
  • Styling of input and keyword elements change depending on correct or incorrect guesses
  • Guessed letters and/or words are displayed
  • Styling of the game changes depending on its outcome (e.g. If won, a new image appears)
